A fearsome sentient creature aligned with the Upside Down, Vecna had humble origins as the human Henry Creel. After moving to a new home in Hawkins in March 1959, the young Henry discovered he had remarkable psychokinetic abilities, and could alter the perception of others. Motivated by a nihilistic and misanthropic philosophy, Henry used his powers to psychologically torture his family, eventually killing his mother and sister, and driving his father to madness.


These events would draw the interest of Dr. Martin Brenner, who took him to Hawkins National Laboratory to be studied; Henry became Brenner's first test subject, and was designated 001. Brenner implanted a substance called 'Soteria' into Henry's neck, disabling his powers. In time, Henry became one of several orderlies who attended the lab, his secret past concealed. Brenner would go on to conduct a range of experiments, replicating Henry's powers in a number of child test subjects, who lived within the lab's confines.
